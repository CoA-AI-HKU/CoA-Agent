## Jockey Club Centre for Positive Ageing 25th Anniversary Celebration: Sweet 25 Years! Ice Cream Truck Surprise Flash Event

To celebrate the 25th anniversary of the Jockey Club Centre for Positive, we recently held a special “Sweet 25 Years” ice cream truck flash event, inviting elderly and public to share in the joy. During the hot summer, we arranged for a classic ice cream truck to serve delicious soft ice cream, allowing elderly to enjoy a sweet time in the heat.

The atmosphere at the event was lively, with participants not only enjoying the ice cream but also eagerly taking photos and receiving a eco-bag for the 25th anniversary, witnessing the growth and development of the Centre over the years.

## Day Care Centre Activity - Steamed Rice Cake Making Workshop

To promote cognitive training and social interaction among service users, we recently held a “Steamed Rice Cake Making Workshop.” This activity aimed to provide multi-sensory stimulation and social interaction opportunities through the making of nostalgic food.

During the event, participants were involved in various stages of making steamed rice cakes, including:

- Soaking and cooking red beans
- Mixing and stirring the rice flour
- Preparing molds and pouring the mixture
- Observing the steaming process
- Unmolding and packaging the finished product

Throughout the activity, our service users not only revisited the traditional food-making process but also practiced fine motor skills through hands-on involvement. During the tasting session, everyone enjoyed their homemade steamed rice cakes while sharing past stories, creating a warm and joyful atmosphere.

Our Centre regularly organizes various cognitive training and social activities, and we welcome public to schedule visits. For more details about the day care service, please visit our official website: [Day Care Service](https://www.jccpa.org.hk/services/day-care-service/).

## Advanced Diploma Programme in Dementia Care is currently accepting applications

This one-year programme organized by the Department of Medicine and Therapeutics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong and co-organised by JCCPA, is currently accepting applications. The objective of the programme is to provide a comprehensive learning platform for professionals in the field or individuals who pursue studies in dementia care. Through different perspectives and cross-disciplinary professional interventions, participants will gain an in-depth understanding of dementia and deliver quality dementia care.

The course duration for the program is from September 2025 to April 2026. The deadline for registration is from now until 11 August, 2025 (Monday). Professional medical and nursing workers providing elderly care services (including social workers, nurses, occupational therapists and physiotherapists), and care professionals interested in a career in dementia care, please visit the following website for more information: [https://www.jccpa.org.hk/en/training/advanced-diploma/](https://www.jccpa.org.hk/en/training/advanced-diploma/).

## New Season of Courses on the EDU Platform Launches

In June 2023, the Centre launched Hong Kong’s first comprehensive online learning platform for dementia: EDU. This platform offers various courses that share knowledge and care skills related to dementia through videos and interactive exercises in an engaging and accessible manner. Users can explore dementia knowledge flexibly anytime and anywhere. Each course provides notes to help users grasp key points and review care tips.

The platform has now launched a new season of course content, offering “Lift and Exercise Techniques.” Interested individuals can learn more and enroll in the courses at：[https://edu.jccpa.org.hk/course/](https://edu.jccpa.org.hk/course/)
