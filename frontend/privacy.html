🔒 Privacy & Consent

私隱政策與同意書

📊 Data Collection & Usage 數據收集與使用

English: CoA-Agent is designed with your privacy as our top priority.

  • We DO NOT store raw conversation text. We only log anonymous, structured metadata (e.g., interaction count, intent category).
  • Your conversations are processed in real-time and not saved to any database.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is required or stored.

中文：CoA-Agent 以您的私隱為最優先考量。

  • 我們不會儲存任何對話原始內容。僅記錄匿名結構化數據（例如：互動次數、問題類別）。
  • 您的對話會即時處理，不會儲存到任何資料庫。
  • 無需亦不會儲存任何個人識別資料（如姓名、電話）。

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer 醫療免責聲明

CoA-Agent is NOT a medical device.

All responses are for informational and supportive purposes only. They do NOT replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

If you are in immediate danger or a medical emergency, call 999 or your local emergency number immediately.

中文：CoA-Agent 並非醫療器材。

所有回應僅供參考及支持用途，不能取代專業醫療建議、診斷或治療。

如有即時危險或醫療緊急情況，請立即撥打 999 或當地緊急熱線。

🛡️ Your Rights 您的權利

  • Withdraw anytime: You can stop using CoA-Agent at any time by simply closing the chat.
  • No data retention: Since we don't store conversation history, there is no data to delete.
  • No profiling: We do not build user profiles or use your data for any purpose other than improving our support response in real-time.
  • 隨時退出：您只需關閉聊天即可隨時停止使用 CoA-Agent。
  • 不留存數據：由於我們不儲存對話紀錄，因此無需刪除任何數據。
  • 不進行用戶畫像：我們不會建立用戶檔案，亦不會將您的數據用於即時支持以外的任何用途。
