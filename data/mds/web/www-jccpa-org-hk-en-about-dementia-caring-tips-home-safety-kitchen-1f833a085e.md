# Hints for Home Safety – Kitchen

Install a smoke detector on the ceiling

Adequate lighting, (600lux) for sink and all table surfaces

Use transparent cabinet door or put item list on door. Avoid putting items on high level

Lock up dangerous items in non transparent cabinets

Put a food list on the refrigerator door to prevent the elder from buying the same item repetitively

Table top with good colour contrast to the floor

Have clear labels on taps eg. red label for hot water and blue label for cold water

Put labels with both names and photos on the drawers

Handle with good color contrast to the background

Non glare, non slippery floor, avoid using placemat or loose cloth on floor

Use electric stove or use gas stove with safety cut off feature. Hide the gas shut off control in a hidden area

Use a thermo boiler or a beeping boiler

Clear simple labels on washer such as a colour contrast label on the on /off button

Clear labels on seasoning containers
