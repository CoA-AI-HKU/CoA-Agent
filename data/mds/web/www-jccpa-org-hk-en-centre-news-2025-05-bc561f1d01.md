## Dragon Boat Festival Event

As the Dragon Boat Festival approaches, our center organized a festive rice dumpling (zongzi) making activity. Many of our residents have experience making zongzi in their younger days, so they handled the task with ease. We were also delighted to welcome members of Wai Yin Association, including Ms. Heidi Chu, Ms. Anne Heung, Ms. Ellen Wong , Ms. Chiu Yi Leung, Ms. Phoebe Pang, Ms. Penny Yeung, and Ms. Maggie Lee, who joined our residents in making zongzi.

This activity not only evoked fond memories of the Dragon Boat Festival and reminded everyone of the festive spirit, but also served as a cognitive exercise, enhancing hand-eye coordination and spatial awareness. Finally, everyone enjoyed tasting their creations, fully immersing themselves in the festive atmosphere

## Cantonese Opera Volunteer Performance

It was honored to invite the Cantonese opera volunteer team to deliver an exciting performance for our elder members. The familiar melodies captivated the elderly, who not only clapped along to the rhythm but also sang together, creating a joyful atmosphere.

We sincerely thank the volunteers for their heartfelt performance, bringing smiles and warmth to our elder members. If any groups are interested in visiting or collaborating on activities, please feel free to contact us to discuss arrangements!

## Advanced Diploma Programme in Dementia Care is currently accepting applications

This one-year programme organized by the Department of Medicine and Therapeutics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong and co-organised by JCCPA, is currently accepting applications. The objective of the programme is to provide a comprehensive learning platform for professionals in the field or individuals who pursue studies in dementia care. Through different perspectives and cross-disciplinary professional interventions, participants will gain an in-depth understanding of dementia and deliver quality dementia care.

The course duration for the program is from September 2025 to April 2026. The deadline for registration is from now until 28 July, 2025 (Monday). Professional medical and nursing workers providing elderly care services (including social workers, nurses, occupational therapists and physiotherapists), and care professionals interested in a career in dementia care, please visit the following website for more information: [https://www.jccpa.org.hk/training/advanced-diploma/](https://www.jccpa.org.hk/training/advanced-diploma/)。

## Training Courses for Dementia Care

With the generous support from the Hong Kong Jockey Club Community Project Grant, JCCPA continues to organise the training programme for caregivers.

The third seminar of this year, titled “Non-Pharmacological Intervention Methods and Effects,” was successfully held on 17th May. It was led by our Service Manager Ms. Vivian Mak. She shared insights with over 222 care partners on non-pharmacological intervention methods and their effects, addressing the psychological and emotional needs of individuals with dementia.

Jockey Club Centre for Positive Ageing regularly organizes care technique courses for caregivers, providing effective solutions and support tailored to their needs and challenges. This aims to alleviate the pressures faced by caregivers and foster mutual understanding and companionship.

## New season of courses for EDU platform has been launched

In June 2023, JCCPA launched the EDU platform, the first comprehensive e-learning platform on dementia in Hong Kong. The platform offers various courses on dementia knowledge and caregiving skills through engaging videos, interactive exercises and in-depth explanations. Users can flexibly and independently access the platform anytime and anywhere to enhance their understanding of dementia. Each course provides course notes, allowing users to grasp key points and review caregiving tips whenever needed.

The platform has currently launched a new season of courses, including “Basic Nursing Care and Rehabilitation” specifically designed for caregivers. Please visit the following website to register if you are interested: [https://edu.jccpa.org.hk/course/](https://edu.jccpa.org.hk/course/)
