## The Launch of EDU, the first comprehensive dementia E-learning platform in Hong Kong

On June 24, 2023, in honor of the 23rd anniversary of JCCPA, we proudly launched EDU, Hong Kong’s first comprehensive e-learning platform dedicated to dementia.

With thanks to technological development, online learning has become a popular trend. In order to build a dementia-friendly community, JCCPA strive to keep pace with the times by providing more training opportunities without time and geographical constraints. Whether you are professional workers, caregivers, corporates, or who are interested in dementia, you are welcome to participate in different courses via this platform. By detailed explanations and real-life examples, you may understand more about dementia.

Starting from fourth quarter of 2023, more courses will be releasing in this platform. To give users a chance to explore our offerings, we will be offering a free trial for the first three months, during which users will have access to a range of videos. We will be announcing our pricing scheme at a later date so that everyone has a chance to experience the platform before making a commitment

Please visit [https://edu.jccpa.org.hk/](https://edu.jccpa.org.hk/) for more information and share with friends in need.

Start your first dementia course in EDU !

## Training Courses for Dementia Care

With the support from the Hong Kong Jockey Club Community Project Grant, JCCPA has launched a new programme “Training Courses for Dementia Care”.

The half-day intensive training course was successfully held on 17 June. Mr. Kingsley Pun, has shared with over 180 caregivers regarding the distressed behavior and psychological symptoms of people with dementia and how to tackle it in short and long term. He also addressed various enquiries from caregivers with practical suggestions during the talk and encouraged caregiver to relieve their stress by chatting with family members and friends.

The next two training courses are scheduled on 15 Jul (Sat) 10am & 1:30pm respectively. Our registered social worker, Ms Ally Lam will introduce basic knowledge of dementia and communication skills on taking care people with dementia etc. For more details and registration, please click the link: [https://www.jccpa.org.hk/s/2023-S3-CG-training](https://www.jccpa.org.hk/s/2023-S3-CG-training)
