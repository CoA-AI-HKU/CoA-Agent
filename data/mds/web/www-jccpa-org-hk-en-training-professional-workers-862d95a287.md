# Professional Workers

JCCPA offers professional training courses from time to time for members of the dementia care sector. By integrating theory and knowledge about dementia, service experience and study results, we seek to help professional workers better understand dementia and contribute to enhancing care skills and service levels.

## Targets

Professionals involved in service and care for people with dementia (including doctors, nurses, occupational therapists, physiotherapists, social workers and personal care workers)
