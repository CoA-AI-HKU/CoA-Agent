# Privacy Policy Statement

## Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance

According to the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, Jockey Club Centre for Positive Ageing has adopted the following policies to protect and safeguard the personal data of the web users. Our organisation will not disclose any personal data to any third parties and adopts the following policies to protect web user’s individual information:

## Basic Statement

Our organisation collects the personal data from web users on voluntary basis. Personal data may include name, title, company, phone number, e-mail address, etc. The personal data our organisation collects is used for the stated purpose of processing donation and using the data as intended.

## Collection and Use

Our organisation’s web server tracks and collects general information about the visits to our organisation’s website. For example, our organisation will compile statistics that show the daily numbers of visitors to our organisation’s site, the daily requests our organisation receives for particular files, the types of browsers used, the visitors’ domain names, and what countries those requests come from.

Our organisation will use “Google Analytics” and record visits to this website without collecting any personal identifiable information of users. Such general statistics are collected for the compilation of statistical reports and the diagnosis of problems with or concerning computer systems to help us improve this website.

When a person visits the website on the computer, a cookie is a small amount of data created in a computer. It often includes an anonymous unique identifier. A cookie can be used to identify a computer. It, however, is not used to collect any personal data. In other words, it does not have the function of identifying an individual user of this website.

The cookies are used by our organisation to collect statistics about the number of visits of users to this website and the users’ preference of websites and online services offered on this website.

You may choose to accept or reject cookies. If you reject the cookies, you will not be able to use some of the functions of this website, such as saving your preferences in using this website and accessing some online services on this website.

When our organisation invites you to provide information, it will specify the purpose and use of the information collected, and will inform you how to request access to or correction of the personal data provided.

## Security of personal data

Access to all personal data provided to is restricted. Only authorized persons may use the personal data you provide in accordance with the purposes for which the information was collected. This website uses the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol to encrypt data during online registration information to ensure that your personal data is protected.

## Revision of the privacy policy statement

This privacy policy statement may be reviewed and changed from time to time. Please contact our organisation or visit our website to obtain the latest privacy policy statement.

## Query / delete provided personal data

If your personal data needs to be modified, corrected or deleted, please contact our organisation through Email: info@jccpa.org.hk

## Links to other URLs

Our organisation is not liable to the content and privacy issues of other websites that may link from our website.

This webpage contains links to other websites. Our organisation tries its best to select links to websites that comply with personal privacy and is not responsible for the content or privacy handling of other websites.

## Opt out of receiving any more mailings

If you do not wish to receive further training courses / services promotion e-mail from us, please write to us or reply by e-mail to info@jccpa.org.hk.

Remarks: If there is any inconsistency or ambiguity between the English version and the Chinese version, the English version shall prevail.
