# Media

JCCPA welcomes media organisations to make use of information and research reports on this website and attribute the information source to the Jockey Club Centre for Positive Ageing.

- 2636 6323
- [ jccpa.org.hk ](http://www.jccpa.org.hk)

We can assist with:

- Introducing JCCPA services
- Arranging interviews with academics and professional colleagues
- Arranging interviews with people with dementia and their caregivers
- Providing the latest information about dementia
- Sharing results of the latest research reports
- Responding to enquiries about dementia

For enquiries, please contact the Department of Communications and Development on 2946 8139.
