# Domestic Helpers

###### Domestic helpers play a major role in care for many families that have a member with dementia. It’s crucial for the helpers to understand dementia and have the right care skills. This course is designed specifically to equip domestic helpers with the care techniques to enhance the quality of life for people with dementia, as well as relieve the care pressure on their employers. The courses are conducted in Cantonese and/or English, with notes in Chinese, English and Bahasa Indonesia.

## Targets

Local and foreign domestic helpers
