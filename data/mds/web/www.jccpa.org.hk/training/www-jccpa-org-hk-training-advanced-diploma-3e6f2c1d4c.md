# 支援腦退化症實務高等文憑課程 - 賽馬會耆智園 | 一站式腦退化症服務中心

###### 本課程由香港中文大學內科及藥物治療學系主辦、賽馬會耆智園協辦，讓業界同工或有志從事腦退化症護理行業人士，從不同角度深入了解腦退化症，以及認識跨專業介入腦退化症護理的實務應用。

## 課程目標

######
- 加強腦退化症護理和服務之實務應用
- 透過跨專業的介入，提升以人為本照顧
- 加強腦退化症照顧的倫理和臨床評估能力
- 個案分析、研究方法及應用
- 發展腦退化症護理相關的社會服務評估

## 修業期

1年制（兩學期）
2026年9月 至 2027年4月

## 對象

專業職系之醫護及安老服務同工
（包括：社工、護士、職業治療師、物理治療師等）、有志從事腦退化症護理行業人士

## 課程結構

ADPDC C1

腦退化症入門：理論和實踐

ADPDC C2

腦退化症護理、腦退化症項目發展

ADPDC C3

實習和個案研究

## 畢業生分享

The course made me realised the crucial importance of person-centred approach to dementia care. The knowledge and skills helped me to develop new awareness and ability. I am feeling reassured, supported and be confident to provide effective strategies while engaging vulnerable or complex care for the people with dementia.

This course facilitates me to attain professionalism and enhance my comprehensive understanding towards dementia, people with dementia and the caregivers. Inspired by this course, I learnt to adopt a more person-centred approach. Understanding is just enough but being empathetic will surely make a difference.

The course enhanced my knowledge and spectrum of management in dementia. Through multidisciplinary model and understanding about person-centred care approach, I am equipped with the capability to formulate a management plan for people with dementia in an effi cient way. The arrangement of different clinical visits further broadened my view on the implementation of person-centred care model for clients with different stages and types of dementia.
